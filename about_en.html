Nosotros

We train professionals oriented to create new computational technologies, as an engine that drives and consolidates the software industries based on scientific and technological research in innovative areas forming in our professionals a set of skills and abilities for the solution of computational problems with a social commitment


Graduate profile

The graduate of the Professional Program of Computer Science is able to:

  • Develop computational technology searching the common weal of individuals, society and organizations.
  • To contribute with its human formation, scientific and professional capacities with the solution of the social problems of our environment.
  • Transform, accelerate and expand the limits of any area of ​​knowledge through innovative solutions based on the efficient use of computational technology.
  • Adapt quickly to technological changes due to their training based on constant research.
  • Work and lead multidisciplinary teams that carry out technological innovation projects.
  • Increase the competitive advantages of any organization through the efficient use of computer technology thanks to its high abstraction capacity.
  • Create technology-based companies
  • To be able to follow postgraduate studies with international requirement in related areas.

Degrees
Cumplida la aprobación de los créditos académicos propuestos en la malla curricular y luego de haber cumplido con los requisitos estipulados en el reglamento de grados y títulos de la carrera, el egresado recibirá:

  • Grado Académico: Bachiller en Ciencia de la Computación y
  • Título Profesional: Licenciado en Ciencia de la Computación.
