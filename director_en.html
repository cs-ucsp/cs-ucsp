Director

Dr. Yván Jesús Túpac Valdivia


Doctor of Electrical Engineering, Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro - Brazil, with experience in Computer Science, emphasis on Computational Intelligence (Neural networks, evolutionary algorithms, fuzzy logic, reinforcement learning, image processing, classification systems) and performance in projects of Research and Development in Petroleum Engineering, Electric Power, Project Evaluation, Risk Analysis, Control and Automation of Processes, Communication Network Systems, Distributed Computing, Electronic and Digital Systems, Analysis and Image Processing.

Active member in the following societies: Society of Petroleum Engineers (SPE), Sociedad Peruana de Computación (SPC), Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers (IEEE), in the Societies of Computing (IEEE-CS) and Computational Intelligence (IEEE-CIS) being chair of the IEEE-CIS Peru Chapter (2017-18). He is also a reviewer of the Journal of Petroleum Science and Engineering (Elsevier), member of the Editorial Board of: Electronic Magazine eCLEI, CompuScientia Magazine (Peru), member of the LA-CCI Steering Committee, CLEI Countries Committee and twice winner of the Petrobras Technology Awards (2005 and 2007 editions).

He has several scientific publications in reputed journals indexed and is scientific reviewer of conferences and publications in his area.
