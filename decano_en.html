Dean of the faculty Engineering and Computing

Dr. Carlos Renzo Zeballos Velarde


Architect and town planner, specialized in planning and urban environmental design and analysis in Geographic Information Systems, with studies in Japan, USA, Argentina and Peru.

He obtained his Doctorate in the University of Kyoto, Japan, as well as a master's degree at the Lanus National University, Buenos Aires, Argentina. He studied Architecture at the National University of San Agustín de Arequipa.

Member of the Editorial Board of the Journal of Civil Engineering and Architecture Research, and professor affiliated with the Federal University of the Far East in Vladivostok, Russia.

He has participated as an exhibitor at conferences in countries on 4 continents and has visited more than 60 countries in 5 continents collecting information on architecture, urbanism and landscape, about which he has taught and written in multiple publications in several countries and languages.

As a consultant he has participated in different plans of importance for the city: Plan of the Historical Center (UNESCO -2000), Master Plan of Arequipa 2002-2015, Pro Chili, Metropolitan Plan of Arequipa 2015-2025. He participated as guest lecturer and lecturer at various national universities. He has published several publications and organized international photo exhibitions.
